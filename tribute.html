Tribute to Lord Krishna

Lord Krishna is one of the most beloved deities in Hinduism. He is considered as the eighth avatar of Lord Vishnu and was born in Mathura to Devaki and Vasudeva. He is also known as Shyam, Gopala, Kanhaiya, and many other names. Lord Krishna is known for his teachings on dharma, karma, and bhakti, which are described in the Bhagavad Gita.

Lord Krishna is often depicted playing the flute, surrounded by cows and gopis. He is also shown as a mischievous child who steals butter and curd from his mother's kitchen. His divine love for Radha is celebrated in the festival of Holi.

Lord Krishna's life and teachings continue to inspire millions of people around the world. His message of love, compassion, and selflessness is timeless and universal.
